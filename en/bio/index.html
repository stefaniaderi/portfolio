Bio

A practice built
around attention.

Stefania D’Eri is an independent graphic designer and musician based in Verona, Italy. Her work moves between printed matter—books, journals and record sleeves—and digital environments for cultural institutions and independent projects. Music is not a parallel activity but another way of working with structure, duration and notation. Across both practices, she builds systems that make room for content rather than decorate it. She works directly with clients and collaborators, taking on a limited number of commissions each season.
